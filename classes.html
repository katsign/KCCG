Classes at KCCG

KC Clay Guild offers classes that accommodate first-timers through advanced potters.

Anyone can learn to throw on the potter's wheel or learn how to work with clay using hand building techniques in one of our classes. Register for a class today to begin working with one of our friendly and skilled instructors.

Class Schedule

All classes begin on the first class day of each month, meet once a week, and are four weeks long.
